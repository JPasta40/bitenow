Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

RushPlate only requests your location when you tap the location button. We do not store your exact location permanently.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your location is used only to show nearby restaurants and provide the fastest, cheapest options. Favorites are saved locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Geoapify for restaurant data and may link to DoorDash or Uber Eats. These services have their own privacy policies.

4. Your Rights

You can clear your favorites anytime by clearing your browser data. We do not collect emails, passwords, or personal accounts at this time.
